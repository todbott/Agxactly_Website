About Us

About

AGXactly is an Agriculture informatics company that helps farmers reduce crop losses and maximize yields by leveraging cutting edge advanced technologies.

We care about your time more than anything and are on a mission to give you all of the benefits from technology without having to spend time administering the tech.

We are currently saving our early adopters in California save $2M+ annually while providing a 10x+ ROI

Vision

To make impactful technology accessible to all farmers worldwide and help the world reach new heights.

Mission

To reduce crop loss rates and put more money in farmer’s pockets while increasing the volume of food in our food supply chain.
