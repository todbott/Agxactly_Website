Online crop-counting interface
Put all flyover images into the Google Drive folder titled "flyoverImages," then select a flight date and Ranch/Block Sublot from the box on the right, and click "Start the count."

There is no need to keep this page open after clicking "Start the count." The count will proceed automatically in the cloud, and the spreadsheet will be updated at the end.
